QCAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

1. Overview

QCAI (provided by Queen City AI, "we", "us", or "our") is an intelligent community assistant application serving residents of Charlotte, North Carolina. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe environment for all our users.

2. Data Collection & Usage

To provide our community services, we collect the following information:

  • Authentication Data: User IDs (usernames/emails) are collected to secure your account and link your posts in the Marketplace, Rentals, and Events sections.
  • Location Information: We use your map location to provide localized content (such as nearby rentals or events). This data is used for application functionality and is not used for tracking.

3. User-Generated Content (UGC) Policy

QCAI allows users to post rental listings, marketplace items, and events. Our commitment to safety includes:

  • Moderation: We maintain a zero-tolerance policy regarding illegal, discriminatory, or offensive content.
  • Enforcement: All reported content is reviewed and, if found in violation, will be removed within 24 hours.
  • Safety Tools: Users have access to in-app reporting and blocking features for every post or profile.

4. AI Smart Assistant — Data Sharing Disclosure

QCAI includes an AI-powered assistant that sends your queries to third-party AI service providers. We are committed to full transparency about how this works.

4a. What data is sent

  • Your typed message — the text you enter in the AI chat input field.
  • Photos you attach — only when you explicitly add an image to a query (e.g., for DIY help or real estate analysis).
  • Topic context — the app feature area (e.g., "Real Estate", "Business", "CLT Vibe") used to craft the AI system prompt. This does not include your name or account details.
  • Language preference — English, Chinese, or Spanish, used to localize the AI response.

4b. Who receives your data

Depending on the AI engine you select in the app's Settings, your messages are sent to one of the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI, Inc. (ChatGPT / GPT-4)
    API endpoint: api.openai.com
    Privacy Policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Google LLC (Gemini)
    API endpoint: generativelanguage.googleapis.com
    Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Both providers are required by their terms of service to protect user data and may not use API inputs to train their models by default. Please review their individual privacy policies for full details.

4c. What is NOT shared

  • Your account email or password
  • Your GPS location or precise home address
  • Your financial, banking, or payment information
  • Your browsing history within the app
  • Any data from other app sections (Events, Marketplace, Rentals) unless you paste it into the chat yourself

5. User Consent & AI Opt-Out

Before you use AI-powered features for the first time, the app will display a consent screen explaining what data is shared and with whom. You must actively agree before any data is sent to third-party AI providers.

  • To grant consent: Tap "I Agree" on the AI consent screen when prompted.
  • To withdraw consent: Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy → Revoke AI Consent. AI chat features will be disabled until you re-enable consent. All other app features remain available.

6. Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. You can manage your profile settings within the app or contact our support team for assistance with data removal.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or support requests can be directed to the QCAI Team.
