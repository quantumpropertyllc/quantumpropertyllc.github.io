DIY your AI Workstation

Complete guide to building local LLM rigs from scratch.

1. Define Your Workload

Inferencing

Running models like Llama 3 or Mistral for chat/tasks.

  • Focus on VRAM (12GB+)
  • Single GPU is enough
  • Balanced CPU/RAM

Fine-Tuning

Training models on your own private data.

  • Multiple GPUs (48GB+ Total)
  • High PSU Wattage (1200W+)
  • Large System RAM (128GB+)

2. Components Checklist

  • The GPU (Most Critical)

    The core of AI calculations. NVIDIA is recommended for CUDA support, though AMD (ROCm) is catching up.

    Top Picks: RTX 4090 (24GB), RTX 4070 Ti (16GB), or used 3090s for budget 24GB.

  • Motherboard & CPU

    CPU keeps GPUs fed. Motherboard must have enough PCIe lanes/slots for multi-GPU builds.

  • Power & Cooling

    AI runs hot. Use high-airflow cases and 80+ Gold/Platinum PSUs with enough overhead (20% above peak).
